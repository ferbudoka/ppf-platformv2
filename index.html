Platform Features

Built for
Infrastructure Professionals

🗺️
Interactive Map Explorer
Visualise all preparation facilities on a live Leaflet map. Filter by region to highlight coverage areas with colour-coded overlays. Switch between cluster, heat, and point views.
⚡
Smart Filtering Engine
Combine region, support type, sector, and financing instrument filters simultaneously. Results update in real time across the map, sidebar, grid, and table views.
📋
Detailed Facility Profiles
Access full eligibility criteria, funding envelopes, deadlines, and sector focus for each facility. Slide-up detail panels keep context without losing your place.
🌐
Multi-language Support
Full interface available in English, Spanish, and French — covering the languages of the regions most served by project preparation finance.
📤
Submit New Facilities
Know of a facility not yet in the database? Use the structured submission form to contribute data. All submissions reviewed within 5–7 business days.
☀️
Light & Dark Modes
Switch between dark cartographic and light analytical themes. Map tiles update automatically to maintain optimal contrast and readability in any environment.
